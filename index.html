The Coca-Cola Company is a multinational beverage corporation, best known for its iconic soft drink, Coca-Cola. It has evolved over the years from the first glass of Coca-Cola poured in 1886 to a "total beverage company" with a portfolio of over 200 brands, including soft drinks, waters, coffee, and teas.
